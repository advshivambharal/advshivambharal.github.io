About

I am Shivam Bharal, an Advocate and Doctor of Philosophy Scholar in Law at the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, with academic and professional engagement in criminal law, constitutional studies, and the interdisciplinary interface between law, technology, and public policy. My work combines doctrinal legal research with comparative and policy-oriented analysis, focusing on criminal justice administration, constitutional rights, artificial intelligence governance, cyber law, and regulatory frameworks in emerging digital societies.
